Privacy Policy

If any significant revisions are made to the policies listed on this page, i post a notice in the What's New section of the Play Store.

Installation of this app/update constitutes acceptance of this policy.

Analytics

MoonWriter uses Firebase Analytics, a service by Google, to collect anonymous usage data such as which features are used most often, how long users spend using the app, device models, Android versions, available memory and storage, and more.

I do not store or collect personal information, such as the owner's name, email address and the exact location of the user.

Opt-out. You may disable analytics by turning off the "Error & Usage Reporting" setting in Settings.

Crash reporting

MoonWriter uses Firebase Crashlytics, a service by Google, to collect crashes and exceptions. Reports may include app logs and info attached to the report, along with info such as device model, Android version, app version, available memory and storage, battery, and more.

I do not store or collect personal information, such as the owner's name, email address and the exact location of the user.

Opt-out. You may disable crash reports by turning off the "Error & Usage Reporting" setting in Settings. Crash reporting will remain active until the app is fully restarted.

Read permission for contacts

MoonWriter should access your Google account (via contacts) in order to access your Google Drive. Access is needed to back up and restore your notes from the cloud. Contacts are not collected, stored, used or sent to a third party.

I do not store or collect personal information, such as the owner's name, email address and the exact location of the user.
